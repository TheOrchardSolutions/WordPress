Equipment Take-In Form

Thank you for choosing the Orchard for your computer repair.

Please take a moment to review our take-in process to make sure your service is as timely as possible.

  • The Orchard currently charges $120 per hour for our services. There is also a one hour minimum charge, which covers our time spent in diagnoses, our collective years of experience, and operating expenses. For computers brought in outside of their warranty period, we require a pre-payment of charges in the amount of $60.
  • It is important to note that we do not charge for any service covered by your AppleCare warranty.
  • In most cases, we ask you to leave only your computer. Unless you think an accessory is related to the problem, your cases, cables, and adapters are best kept in your possession.
  • We will diagnose the computer shortly after drop off, and can provide same-day diagnoses for computers brought in before 3PM. Once we have diagnosed the issue, we will contact you with a quote detailing the expected charges.

We look forward to helping!
