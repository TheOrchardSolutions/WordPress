Introducing plum.Shop

The unbelievably easy-to-use, feature-rich shopping cart powered by jQuery

plum.Shop is eCommerce at its roots. It’s designed to be completely hassle-free and still offer enough options to get into the nitty gritty of your online store.

Plum has over 30 configuration options, including multiple checkout methods, currency locales, discount codes, tax exempt and shipping exempt settings, callback functions and more. It sports a rich API to create a flawless integration with any layout.

It’s fast, it’s powerful, and it’s exquisitly packaged into a file that's under 20 Kb. And if you use gzip, it’s under 6 Kb.

Design your cart the way you want it to appear. There are no restrictions on HTML or required class selectors. You tell Plum what you want it to do.

plum.Shop is pure JavaScript; it doesn’t need a database or server scripting (though you can of course use such if you need). That means it’s lightning fast and can be used with flat HTML files. If Amazon FPS, Google Checkout, PayPal, and Moneybookers aren’t enough for you, you can use the custom checkout method to define your own.
